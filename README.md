# How-you-Identify-and-implement-scalable-recruiting-improvements:
1. Unclear Role Definition
Impact: Recruiters source the wrong profiles, interviews drag on, and the pipeline resets repeatedly.
2. Misalignment on Level or Compensation
Impact: Strong candidates drop early, or the search becomes unrealistic.
3. Slow or Inconsistent Feedback
Impact: Candidates lose interest, accept other offers, or the process stalls.
4. Overly Complex or Unstructured Interview Process
Impact: Long timelines, candidate fatigue, and inconsistent hiring decisions.
5. Low yield Sourcing Strategy
Impact: Weak pipeline, repeated re sourcing, and wasted time.
6. Lack of Hiring Manager Engagement
Impact: Recruiter works in the dark, and the search drags on.
7. Candidate Experience Issues
Impact: Top candidates drop out early.
8. Internal Process Bottlenecks
Impact: Even when the right candidate is found, the process stalls.
